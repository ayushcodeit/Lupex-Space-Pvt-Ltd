Who We Are

Pioneering the next generation of space leaders

Lupex Space Pvt Ltd (formerly Mach One India) is on a singular mission: to democratize aerospace education across every school and college in India. We don't just teach theory — we build real labs, deliver expert training, and create hands-on aerospace experiences that transform students into builders.

Founded in 2025, we are the bridge between India's 1.5 million schools and the boundless frontier of space. Every institution we enter becomes a launchpad for the next generation of aerospace innovators.
